Shanghai DimSum

Welcome to Shanghai DimSum

Shanghai DimSum provides the most authentic Shanghai dishes in GTA. Our signature, "Xiao Long Bao" (also known as Soup Dumpling) dates back over a century and still remains a culinary icon of Shanghai today.

Though across the oceans, our soup dumplings still maintain the highest production standards: selected high-quality flour, pork, carb, fresh ingredients, no added artificial colors, made by Shanghai's famous chef daily.

In addition to an extensive selection of "Xiao Long Bao", we also offer classic, delicate and authentic Shanghai homely dishes. Shanghai tour begins on the tongue.
